Want the Olé Show Dates on the GO?

 

HERE'S HOW!

 

For iPhone

Step 1: Go the theolesband.com on your iPhone and tap the Show Dates link

 

Step 2: Click on the arrow at the bottom of your screen, and then select "Add to Home Screen"

 

Enter any name you would like for the "app bubble" and Voilà! Now it's on your home screen! Tap it to go straight to the page and see the latest show dates.
