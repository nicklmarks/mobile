The Olé's - The Game

 

Due to publishing liabilities and difficulties, the release date of "The Olé's - The Game" has been postponed to a couple weeks after the originally mentioned time of May 20th.

Dom is currently working out the kinks and fighting off vultures who are trying to buy/steal the idea of the game from him. Stay tuned for the release of The Olé's - The Game!

 

 

Controls

Left/Right Arrow Keys = Move
Up Arrow Key = Jump
R = Reset Level
in case you somehow fall off the map)

 

How The Scoring Works

1) You start the game with 1000 points
2) Every second that goes by, you lose a point
3) Every time you die, you lose 5 points
4) You DO NOT lose points when you reset a level. You are already penalizing yourself doing that, we aren't that mean.
5) At the end of the game, your total score is what points you have left.

So, for all of you wanting to get on the High Scores List (you can submit your score at the end of the game), the one key tip we will give is that you need to finish each level as fast as you can without dying. The rest is up to you to find out!
